First results calculated and presented in July 1993 in Berlin.

In November 1993 the first paper was submitted and thereupon another which used downcoverted photons.

We wrote the first paper independently and unaware of M. Zukowski, A. Zeilinger, M. A. Horne, and A. K. Ekert, Phys. Rev. Lett. 71, 4287 (1993) and C. H. Bennett, C. Brassard, C. Crepau, R. Jozsa, A. Peres. and W. K. Wooters, Phys. Rev. Lett. 70, 1895 (1993).

The results of all three groups are equivalent and were confirmed experimentally in J.-W. Pan, D. Bouwmeester, H. Weinfurter, and A. Zeilinger, Phys. Rev. Lett. 80, 3891 (1998). The entanglements can also be interpreted as teleportations of photon states.
