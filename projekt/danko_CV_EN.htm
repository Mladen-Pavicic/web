Social skills and competencies

Communication and discussions that often result in a support given by individuals and institutions. For example, in mid nineteen eighties I started organizing a series of biannual conferences for scientists from widely different branches of physics and chemistry on the Brijuni Island (http://www.irb.hr/users/bosanac/motif.htm) and I succeeded in getting an ever growing support from recognized scientists (including Nobel Prize winners), scientific institutions, Ministry of science, local authorities, and since 2002 it became a NATO Advanced Research Workshop.
