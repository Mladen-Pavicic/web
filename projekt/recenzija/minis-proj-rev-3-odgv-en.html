Dear Professor Pavicic,

we have considered your statement on the report of the Scientific Board and we want to inform you that the evaluation procedure of scientific-project and -programme proposals has been carried out according to the standards for selection of scientific projects and programmes that are in complete accordance with the evaluation procedure in the countries of the European Union.

Scientific Boards, completely independent of the Ministry, carried out the whole evaluation procedure. Consequently, based on the instructions to referees and Scientific Boards (Evaluation Groups), accepted by the National Council for Science and available on the Ministry web pages scientific projects, the Scientific Board for Physics rejects the project with particular recommendations to the head of the project.

Your submission of the revised version of the project will be evaluated as all the other submissions of scientific-project and -programme proposals.

Yours sincerely
